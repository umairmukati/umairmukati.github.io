M. Umair Mukati

I am an Early Stage Researcher in Marie Curie RealVision ITN, where I am conducting research broadly on the topic of light field coding and processing.

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Photonics Engineering in Technical University of Denmark and a part of Coding and Visual Communication group headed by Prof. Søren Forchhammer. I received my B.E. degree in Industrial Electronics Engineering from NED University of Engineering and Technology, Karachi, Pakistan and M.S. degree in Electrical-Electronics Engineering from Istanbul Medipol University, Istanbul, Turkey in 2014 and 2017, respectively.
